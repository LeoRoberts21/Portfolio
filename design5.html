BBC EDGE

UX/UI • Front-End

A week-long UX brief. I was tasked with designig a product that would benefit student life.

Sketchbook

Approach

This was my first time designing UX/UI. So I wanted to keep it fairly simple and make sure I did the basics right. I had also never used Adobe XD before so discovering app prototyping and wireframing was a really interesting learning experience.

Outcome

I designed a short-form content news app, targetting students with damaged attention spans from extensive social media use. This project tested my ability to work under pressure and get a project within the deadline. It allowed me to test my UX/UI design skills and get even more familiar with the process of researching, designing, wireframing, and testing a product. The majority of this project was conceptualised using Adobe XD.
