Spotify Audiobooks

UX/UI • Campaign

Brief set by 2022 Young Ones Student Awards. Launching audiobooks on Spotfiy to a Gen-z and millennial audience.

Sketchbook

Approach

I wanted to focus on locations throughout this project. Locations younger generations frequent. Where they enjoy going. Where they would rather escape to. With this in mind I started thinking about how I could tailor my marketing to specific areas to make it more fun, relatable and effective.

Outcome

I created a series of posters. These are complimented by similar TV adverts, social media marketing and out of house marketing. The campaign uses the #thegreatescape, and aims to give viewers as sense of the escape from the busy real world they would feel if they were to listen to audiobooks on Spotify.
