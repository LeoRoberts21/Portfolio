Agma.

UX/UI • Branding

A brief challenging me to research previous explorers, select a target audience and create a piece of communication design that captures the content in a new and exciting way.

Sketchbook

Approach

The explorer I decided to base this project around is an agoraphobic photographer called Jacqui Kenney. She travels the world from the comfort of her own home, using google maps. She got me thinking about all the others who wish to explore the world but can’t for various reasons.

Outcome

My final outcome was a brand that brings the holiday experience to those unable to trave. I did this by designing a few touch points. A package that has things like: CD’s, cookbooks, card games, and a map that can be scanned, creating an AR experience . I also designed a website using Figma.
