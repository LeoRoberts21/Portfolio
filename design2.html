Netflix D&AD Brief

Editorial • Branding • Campaign

D&AD competition brief. Design a festival aiming to get UK audiences to watch and talk about non english language films and series on Netflix.

View Sketchbook

Approach

Design a foreign film festival that travels around the UK introducing more people to Foreign film / TV. I wanted to focus on the misconception amongst UK audiences that subtitles ruin films because they are a chore to read, making foreign movies less enjoyable to watch.

Outcome

All the branding is in different languages, with an English subtitle below. I hoped to make viewers more comfortable with subtitles and break the negative connotations they hold. The festival would travel around the country on wheels with cinema rooms in the back of lorries. I designed all the branding, a zine, social media marketing and UI mockups.
